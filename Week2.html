    3D printing and scaning

    3D printing
    For 3D printing we use Ultimaker Cura: Advanced 3D printing software.
    Once I designed the joint, it was necessary to save each body as STL.
    After that, we have to open them in Ultimaker Cura.
    Depending on the 3D printer available, it is necessary to select it in the program, and check which is the nozzle correspondant.
    Other thing we have to thought about it is the form of our bodies and if it is necessary to use supports. In my case, it was necessary in all of them. For putting it, we have to select support and configurate them.

    3D scaning

    We have learn how to make 3D scaning.

    For this purpose, we have an iPad with iSense 3D scanner.


    First, it is necessary to focus with the camera the part we would like to scan. After that it appears a square, which it is possible to move and ajust. When we have it, we start scaning. It has to be really slow, but the program will said to us if we are going to quickly.
